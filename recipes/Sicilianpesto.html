Sicilian Tomato Pesto

Description

This Sicilian tomato pesto is a delicious and versatile sauce that can be used in a variety of dishes. It's made with sun-dried tomatoes, almonds, garlic, and olive oil, creating a rich and flavorful condiment.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil
  • 1/2 cup almonds, toasted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. In a food processor, combine the sun-dried tomatoes, almonds, garlic, and basil. Pulse until finely chopped.
  2. With the processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, stir in the grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Transfer the pesto to a jar and store it in the refrigerator for up to a week.
  5. Use it as a pasta sauce, sandwich spread, or dip for vegetables.
